About Us

UA ACM is the leading Computer Science club here at The Capstone. We pride ourselves on our ability to foster a passion for the world of computer science as well as give you the tools to thrive in the industry post-graduation!

Professionalism

Bringing in companies from across the country, we put you in touch with industry members that provide valuable knowledge on how to best succeed in a professional environment as a CS grad.

Coding

Whether it be coding workshops, hackathon opportunities, or competitive programming competitions, ACM provides several different ways for you to be the best coder you can be.

Social

ACM puts you in touch with other Computer Science and Cybersecurity majors on campus, allowing you to build connections that ensure you don't have to go through college alone.

Fall 2024 Events

Below is a tentative schedule of events planned for ACM in Fall 2024.

Fall Get On Board Day - 8/29

Stop by our booth at the school's activity fair to learn more about our plans for the upcoming semester and get a chance to meet our leadership.

Camgian - 9/3

We're welcoming a representative from Camgian, a local software company known for building AI and ML applications for the world's greatest industrial challenges.

Career Fair Prep - 9/10

Come hear from an Engineering Career Development Center rep on preparing for the Career Fair and how to give yourself the best chance to succeed.

Lockheed Martin - 9/19

Learn more about opportunities for Computer Science, Cybersecurity, and Computer Engineering students at Lockheed Martin, a leader in global defense.

Shipt - 10/1

ACM is welcoming a recruiter from Shipt in Birmingham to talk about their business and internship opportunities along with UA students who have interned there previously.

Research Presentation - 10/15

Hear from a UA professor on the exciting research they're engaging in on campus and how you, as a student, could potentially be involved!

Resume Review feat Resumes.fyi - 10/29

Hear from ACM's very own Brandon Kantorinski as he delivers his tips on crafting the perfect resume and promotes an exciting new platform, Resumes.fyi.

Clash of Code - 11/12

We're closing out the semester with a mini coding compeition we're calling the Clash of Code! Compete in a variety of programming challenges to win a grand prize at the end.

Crimson Crossover Powered By Camgian

ACM is proud to host, in collaboration with the Crimson Defense Cybsersecurity Club, the Crimson Crossover Powered By Camgian!

The Crimson Crossover Powered By Camgian is a first of its kind combination of competitive programming and cybersecurity capture the flag to decide who among ACM and Crimson Defense is the superior computing club!

Team Andrew Salmon were your winners of the inaugural Crimson Crossover Powered by Camgian competing for ACM!

Stay tuned at this page for more information in the Spring on the 2025 iteration of Crimson Crossover Powered By Camgian.
